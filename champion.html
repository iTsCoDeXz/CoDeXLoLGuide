Abilities

Passive: Deathbringer Stance

Periodically, Aatrox's next basic attack deals bonus physical damage and heals him, based on the target's max health.

Q: The Darkin Blade

Aatrox slams his greatsword down, dealing physical damage. He can swing three times, each with a different area of effect.

W: Infernal Chains

Aatrox smashes the ground, dealing damage to the first enemy hit. Champions and large monsters have to leave the impact area quickly or they will be dragged to the center and take the damage again.

E: Umbral Dash

Passively, Aatrox heals for a portion of the damage he deals. On activation, he dashes in a direction.

R: World Ender

Aatrox unleashes his demonic form, fearing nearby enemy minions and gaining increased movement speed, attack damage, and healing. If he gets a takedown, this effect is extended.

Champion Tips

Laning Phase

  • Use your Q to poke enemies from a safe distance.
  • Save your E for escaping ganks or engaging when your jungler comes to gank.
  • Try to hit the sweet spot of your Q for maximum damage and crowd control.

Teamfights

  • Activate your ultimate before engaging to maximize your effectiveness.
  • Try to hit multiple enemies with your Q's sweet spots for AoE damage and crowd control.
  • Use your W to chain crowd control or peel for your carries.

General Tips

  • Practice your Q combos to maximize your damage output.
  • Remember that your E can be used during your Q animation for repositioning.
  • Your passive gives you sustain in lane, so try to proc it on enemy champions when possible.

In-Game Strategy

Early Game (0-10 minutes)

  • Focus on farming and poking your opponent with Q.
  • Buy Doran's Blade or Corrupting Potion as your starting item.
  • At first back, aim for a Caulfield's Warhammer or Phage, depending on your build path.

Mid Game (10-20 minutes)

  • Complete your first core item (Goredrinker or Eclipse).
  • Start roaming to help your team in skirmishes and objectives.
  • Focus on taking down the enemy carries in teamfights.

Late Game (20+ minutes)

  • Complete your build with items like Black Cleaver, Sterak's Gage, and Guardian Angel.
  • Split push when your ultimate is down, group with your team when it's up.
  • Look for flank opportunities to engage on the enemy backline.
